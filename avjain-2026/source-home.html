Welcome to

Shri Aatm Vallabh Jain Girls (P.G.) College

Shri Aatm Vallabh Jain Girls (P.G.) College was established in the year 2000 with merely 42 girl students.

Shri Aatm Vallabh Jain Shiksha Nyas concerted a noble idea and started the college specifically to encourage and foster higher education and learning amongst the girls.

The college offers U.G. & P.G. Courses in the streams like Arts, Science, Computer Application, Management & Commerce.

The College is affiliated to Maharaja Ganga Singh University. The college has come up with the integrated courses like B.A. B.Ed. & B.Sc. B.Ed. from 2017-18.
