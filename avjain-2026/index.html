Legacy

Built to expand higher education for girls in Sri Ganganagar.

According to the live site, the college was established in 2000 with 42 girl students and was started to encourage and foster higher education and learning among girls.

Today the institution presents undergraduate and postgraduate options across Arts, Science, Computer Application, Management, and Commerce, with integrated teacher-education pathways added from 2017-18.
